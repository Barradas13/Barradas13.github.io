# Trigonometria - complexos

## 1)

![Figura 1](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Barradas13/Barradas13.github.io/main/source/resources/tarefa1.png)

$$
\sin\left(\frac{\pi}{6}\right)=\frac{1}{2}=\frac{b}{2}  
\quad \therefore \quad  
b=1  
$$

$$
\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{6}\right)=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}=\frac{a}{2}  
\quad \therefore \quad  
a=\sqrt{3}  
$$

$$
z=\sqrt{3}+i  
$$

## 2)

![Figura 2](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Barradas13/Barradas13.github.io/main/source/resources/tarefa2.png)

$$
\frac{5\pi}{6}=150^\circ  
$$

$$
\sin 150^\circ=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}  
$$

$$
\cos 150^\circ=-\frac{1}{2}  
$$

$$
z=6\left(-\frac{1}{2}+i\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)  
$$

$$
z=-3+i\,3\sqrt{3}  
$$
